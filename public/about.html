Competitor Story

Lucky is a five-year-old male cat. He is very intelligent and can read people. He's very clingy and wants to be in your arms almost every minute. He's very jealous, and if you pet another cat, he'll hiss, and then act pathetic, so you can't beat him. He is very brave, never afraid of strangers, will take the initiative to approach and play.

Bing is a quiet little female cat. She doesn't meow or pester you. She looks at you until you pay attention to her. Her body is softer than a male cat, but she does not like anyone to hold her, like a cold little princess. She is a glutton and always eats until she vomits.

Year is a very powerful little male cat, he likes to play with people, as long as you make him unhappy, and even bite you, but this is all play, you are not very painful. He likes to drag the blanket on the ground, as if to carry the blanket to wander the world.
